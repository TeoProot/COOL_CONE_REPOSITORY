Submit by going to this site and answer the questions with the name you would like for your game(or a link to a picture[or both!]), the link to the game, and your full name. You will be credited for your contribution on the Credits Page of COOL_CONE_GAMES.

ABOUT

COOL_CONE_GAMES is a free gaming website created in early 2025 by Teo Nacamulli Tabet. It features a large range of games, everywhere from clickers to platformers to sports games, many of which are found by users! The games are always unblocked, and are meant to be played anywhere, and anytime. Teo N. Tabet started getting intrested in programming when he was around 8 years old, when he discovered the website Scratch®. By age 10, he was invested in programming and had started the language Python®. In December, 2024, he discovered html and website creation through w3schools.com. He started building this website in January 2024. It quickly became a bigger project, so he started to add CSS and Javascript attributes. Teo N. Tabet continues to make games and other programs. Thank you for playing!
